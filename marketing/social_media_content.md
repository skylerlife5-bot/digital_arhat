## 3. TIKTOK / REELS SCRIPT (30 Seconds)

### Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

**Scene 1 (0-5 seconds): HOOK**
- **Visual:** Farmer on phone scrolling through traditional mandi chaos (crowds, shouting)
- **Urdu Voiceover:** "ہر روز منڈی میں سہی قیمت نہیں ملتی... 😞"
- **On-Screen English Caption:** "Getting scammed at the mandi? 🤔"
- **Music:** Upbeat, trending audio starting

**Scene 2 (5-10 seconds): PROBLEM**
- **Visual:** Shows confused trader looking at price board, multiple conflicting prices, hand gestures
- **Urdu Voiceover:** "کون سی قیمت سچی ہے؟ کون سے سودا پورا ہوگا؟"
- **On-Screen English Caption:** "Which price is real? Which deal will work?"
- **Music:** Builds tension slightly

**Scene 3 (10-15 seconds): SOLUTION INTRO**
- **Visual:** Phone screen lights up, app icon appears with glow effect
- **Urdu Voiceover:** "ڈیجیٹل ارہت... آپ کا حل!"
- **On-Screen English Caption:** "Meet Digital Arhat! 🚀"
- **Music:** Upbeat resolution

**Scene 4 (15-22 seconds): SOLUTION IN ACTION**
- **Visual:** Fast-paced transitions showing:
  - 🌾 Ghar Baithe Rates (Live rates at home)
  - 💰 Sahi Qeemat (Real prices for wheat, rice, chicken)
  - 📊 Trends Dekho (See market trends)
  - ✅ Secure Deals (Transparent transactions)
- **Urdu Voiceover:** "لائیو گندم کی قیمتیں، چاول کے ریٹس، مرغی کی فوری اپڈیٹ! سب کچھ ایک جگہ۔"
- **On-Screen English Caption:** "Live rates. Real prices. Smart trading!"
- **Music:** Peak energy, fast cuts

**Scene 5 (22-28 seconds): CTA**
- **Visual:** App download screen overlay + farmer smiling with thumbs up
- **Urdu Voiceover:** "ابھی ڈاؤن لوڈ کرو! بالکل مفت!"
- **On-Screen English Caption:** "Download NOW - 100% FREE!"
- **Music:** Signature sound/catchphrase "Digital Arhat!" with sound effect

**Scene 6 (28-30 seconds): BRAND CLOSE**
- **Visual:** App logo with tagline animation
- **Text on Screen:** "DIGITAL ARHAT - Pakistan Ka Apna Mandi System"
- **Music:** Ends with brand sound signature

### Audio Requirements:
- **Urdu Voiceover Gender:** Female (friendly, relatable mother/sister tone)
- **Delivery Speed:** Fast, energetic, conversational
- **Post-Production:** Add trending background music (e.g., Pakistani folk + modern beat mix)

## NOTES FOR MARKETING TEAM

1. **Bilingual is KEY:** Always post both Urdu and English on major platforms
2. **Farmer Language:** Use simple, direct words. Avoid jargon.
3. **Urgency Messaging:** "آج ڈاؤن لوڈ کریں" (Download Today!) works better than "جب چاہو"
4. **Visual Consistency:** Use app brand colors (green + gold) across all posts
5. **Mobile-First:** All content optimized for vertical viewing
6. **Local Proof:** Use Lahore references first, then expand to other cities
7. **Testimonials:** Real farmer photos/videos will 5x your engagement
